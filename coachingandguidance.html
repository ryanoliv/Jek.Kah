One-on-one meditation container

This is a personal journey of meditation tailored to your individual needs and unique energy. You work directly with Jessica. Her coaching method is sculpted specifically to help you cultivate a sustainable practice. She draws influence from her own meditation journey over the last 10 years. Borrowing teachings From her experiences from her personal healing journey with meditation being the core of her healing as well as Vipassana meditation and being a qualified Maxx Meditation system guide.

Your journey starts with a meditation introduction through mindfulness and breath. This can be personally tailored to your availability and requirements.

Empower: one-on-one coaching

Jessica takes a unique approach to each individual client. Her process toward personal empowerment is achieved through physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual healing modalities.

Jessica holds an innate love and zeal for helping others find their connection with their inner infinite potential. Through her personal meditation, psychotherapy, coaching and spiritual healing, Jessica creates the space where her clients can dive into the depths of their beings and learn how to harness an exciting and empowered life through nutrition, wellbeing, and alignment.

Jessica aims to introduce a new perspective on life's challenges, situations, and issues. With a background in recovery, self-help, personal growth, and healing modalities, she can create an individual path for each person.

She works with you in narrowing down a core goal or aim and creates a lifestyle plan to help you achieve it and holds your hand while going through it all. Changing your life around can be deep and icky sometimes as Jessica knows herself.
