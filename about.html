About Jessica

I am a International Life Activation Practitioner, Healer and Chef. I embody the belief that you can access your highest self, abundance, joy empowerment through alternative and natural methods. I focus on on energy work, meditation as methods of spiritual healing, life empowerment, manifestation and alignment.

My innate passion is to guide and empower individuals in finding their unique path and to live their desired reality. I help people from all walks of life break through their barriers, blocks, and traumas. With many practices focusing on the integration of physical, mental, emotional and spiritual energy in order to love, heal and accept oneself.

My sensitivity has turned into my greatest strength. As I grew to understood myself better, I realised she held an innate gift for healing. Today, I have learnt and continue to learn how to harness these skills into tools to craft self-love and to hold space for others on their journey. I have faced many challenges such as addiction, substance abuse, sexual abuse, body dysmorphia, suicidal ideation, obsessive compulsive disorder, disordered eating, abortion, codependency and childhood trauma. I have overcome these challenges in life through the healing modalities I offer.

I found help in recovery and, in 2015, starting my healing journey with both inpatient and outpatient recovery assistance. While in recovery, I found my strength in healing, public speaking, and leadership. I became chairwoman for large groups of people on sensitive, empowering and traumatic topics; helping others from every walk of life to find light in their own journey. My experiences in life have given me a voice in which I choose to communicate to the public about topics that are deep and real with compassion, experience and understanding.

Through my journey I have realised how hugely we are impacted by not only what we feed our mind but also what we feed our body. I weave my passion for body nourishment into my teachings, coaching, and healing. Helping one cultivate a healing relationship with food and their body.

Healing has completely changed my life. I feel empowered and I am grateful to be walking the path and journey that I am on. I wish to help you transform yours. To your desired and empowered reality.

There is a healer in all of us. It is our divine right. It belongs to us. Allow me to help guide you in embracing yours.
