About Jessica

Jessica Rea is a Life Activation Practitioner, Meditation Teacher, Ensofic Reiki & SG Crystal Healer, certified through the Modern Mystery School. She’s an intuitive Healer and coach by nature who teaches mindfulness & meditation. Jessica embodies the belief that you can access infinite power through alternative and natural methods. As a Healer, she focuses on energetic & spiritual healing as well as life empowerment. Her innate passion is to assist individuals in finding their unique path toward self-discovery. Jessica helps people from all walks of life break through their barriers, blocks, and traumas. With many practices focusing on the integration of our masculine and feminine energy in order to love, heal and accept oneself.

Being a highly sensitive empath at such a young age proved to be difficult, yet over the years, her sensitivity turned into her strength. As she grew and understood herself better, she realised she held an innate gift for healing. Today, she has learned to harness these skills into tools to craft self-love and the ability to hold space for others on their journey. Jessica has healed & grown through many challenges and traumas such as eating disorders, body dysmorphia, substance abuse, addictive behaviours, codependency and bullying in her younger years. She believes by growing through these she has learnt important tools along with her studied skills to guide you through too. She found help in recovery and, in 2015, started her healing journey with both inpatient and outpatient recovery assistance. While in recovery, she found her strength in healing, public speaking, and leadership. Jessica became chairwoman and speaker for large groups of people on sensitive, empowering and traumatic topics; helping others from every walk of life to find light in their own journey. Her experiences in life have given her a voice in which she chooses to use to speak to the public about topics that are deep & real with compassion.

Jessica enhances her guidance with her culinary knowledge & experience. Through her journey she has realised how hugely we are impacted by not only what we feed our mind but also what we feed our body. She weaves her passion for body nourishment into her teachings, coaching, and healing. Helping one cultivate a healing relationship with food & their body.

Healing has completely changed her life. Now, she wishes to help you transform yours. With meditation, habit building & energy work being the core of her healings & guidance.

There is a healer in all of us. It is our divine right. It belongs to us. Allow me to help guide you in embracing yours.
