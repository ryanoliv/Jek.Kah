Events & Workshsops

Express: Public Speaking & Event hosting

Public Speaking:

Jessica has extensive experience in hosting and facilitating meetings and workshops. Many of her specialised topics include freeing the mind from limiting beliefs, thought patterns, attachments, and addictions. Jessica has been holding space for people to step up and voice their inner truth and pain for many years. With a strong belief in expression and creativity within a safe space, she truly believes in breaking through social barriers and getting to the core of each individual trauma. She would love to get involved in any holistic health, meaning & wellness events. She also offers herself as a volunteer to any charities and organisations who need motivational speakers with focus on experience, strength, hope & healing.

Events:

Jessica has been involved in the music festival and event industry. With a core understanding of logistics and a knack for innovation and creativity, Jessica can bring your event to life and cultivate an experience no guest will forget. She offers herself as a host, event manager, vendor & stall organization or a team member.
