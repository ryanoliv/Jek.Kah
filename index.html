Jessica Réa

There are layers to healing; it isn't simply a quick fix. It is a journey.

Jessica is an International Life Activation Practitioner and Healer. She offers activations, healings, retreats, workshops, coaching and meditations globally (in person and online).

The core of her practice is The Life Activation which is a powerful healing modality that enables you to access your potential in life and allows you to see and progress in your journey of growth. It reawakens the dormant parts of your DNA and reintroduces you to yourself. The activation brings up all elements within yourself that need to be illuminated. A Trajectory shift - A life alignment - an empowerment.

Through activations and energy you are able to release blockages and limiting beliefs that are holding you back from living your desired reality. Receiving healings, activations and spiritual guidance allows you shift the resistance holding you back from your full potential. All of Jessica's services allow you to integrate new energy and empower yourself to take the right steps forward in living a purposeful, joyful and empowered life.
